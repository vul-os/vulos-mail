Mail, Calendar & Contacts

Vulos Mail

Private email, calendar and contacts — fast, clean, and yours.

Vulos Mail is a modern webmail with a built-in calendar and contacts, backed by the lilmail engine and the open, self-hostable Vulos suite. Your password never leaves the server: sessions broker your mailbox credentials so the browser holds nothing sensitive.

One inbox, everything in reach

A focused mail client that brings your schedule and people along.

Email that's fast

A responsive three-pane mailbox: browse, search, flag, and compose with attachments and rich text — all over a clean /v1 JSON API.

Calendar built in

A CalDAV-backed calendar lives right alongside your mail at /calendar, sharing the same secure session — no extra sign-in.

Contacts that follow you

CardDAV contacts at /contacts keep your address book in sync with every message you send.

Credentials never leave the server

Sign-in mints an HttpOnly session that brokers your mailbox password to the engine on each request. The browser never holds it.

Apps & Bots

Extend your mailbox with the shared Vulos Apps & Bots platform, managed from Settings and gated to your account.

Open & self-hostable

Part of Vulos — combine Mail under one login alongside Talk, Meet, Office and more, or run it standalone.

Part of Vulos

Vulos is an open, self-hostable web OS and app suite. Vulos Mail runs perfectly standalone — and can be combined under one login alongside Talk, Meet, Office, and more by Vulos Workspace.
